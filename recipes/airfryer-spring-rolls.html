Airfryer Spring Rolls

Description

Spring rolls are a fantastic option for parties and potlucks, but also a great choice for an easy snack to reheat whenever you feel peckish. This recipe in particular, uses an airfryer rather than traditional oil frying, which makes it just as crunchy but also much more healthy in comparison. They are great to cook in bulk and convenient to store for leftovers later.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs Ground Pork
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Carrot
  • 1 clove Garlic
  • 1 tsp Sesame Oil
  • 2 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Pepper
  • 1 Egg
  • Cooking oil (to brush)
  • Spring Roll Wraps (You can usually buy them frozen in packs of 25-50)

Steps

  1. Mince onions, heat oil in a pan and cook until tender
  2. Mince carrots and garlic
  3. Add ground pork to pan and brown (around 5 mins)
  4. Add minced vegetables, sesame oil, salt and pepper, stir for a few minutes, then remove from heat
  5. Scramble egg (do not cook), put raw in bowl
  6. Wrap the Spring Rolls:
    1. Place wrap on flat surface, with a corner facing you
    2. Put spoonful of filling about halfway between the tip closest to you and the centre of the wrap
    3. Fold corners on the side, and then roll tightly
    4. Seal by brushing egg on the far corner and rolling
    5. Brush a thin layer of oil over the entire spring roll
    6. Set aside (you can cook a portion while wrapping the rest)
  7. Preheat airfryer to 370F
  8. Cook batch of spring rolls for 15 mins, turning halfway, adjust for levels of crispiness
  9. Serve!
